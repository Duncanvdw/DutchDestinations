What Is DutchDestinations?

Welcome to DutchDestinations, your ticket to a personalized Amsterdam adventure! We are passionate about unlocking the magic of this historic city for every explorer, and we believe that the best way to experience Amsterdam is at your own pace.

Who Are We?

We are a team of travel enthusiasts and Amsterdam locals dedicated to transforming your visit into an unforgettable journey. We understand that every traveler is unique, and we've crafted an online self-guided tour that puts you in the driver's seat of your Amsterdam exploration.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide you with the tools and information to explore Amsterdam authentically. We want to break free from the confines of traditional guided tours and empower you to create your own adventure. Amsterdam is a city with stories waiting to be discovered, and we're here to help you uncover them on your terms.

Why Choose DutchDestinations

  • Flexibility: No more rigid schedules or rushing to keep up. Our self-guided tour allows you to start, pause, and continue your journey whenever and wherever you like.
  • Personalization: Tailor your experience to match your interests. Choose the attractions that resonate with you, and let our app guide you through a curated itinerary of your own making.
  • Smart Technology: Our cutting-edge app combines smart routing with interactive maps and real-time navigation, ensuring a seamless and efficient exploration of Amsterdam.
  • Local Insights: We're not just a tour guide; we're your local companion. Discover hidden gems, off-the-beaten-path treasures, and the pulse of Amsterdam from a unique perspective.
